Travel Packages

We offer a wide variety of travel packages to help you get the most out of your trip to Spain. Take a look at some of our most popular packages below! If any of these packages interest you, head over to our Contact Us page and give us a call. You will be able to talk to one of our experienced agents and even get discounted rates!

Madrid

Do you like the hustle and bustle of a big city? Do you want to party all night in a city that never sleeps? If so, Madrid is the place for you! Madrid is the capital of Spain and is the country's largest city. Spend the day exploring the Royal Palace or the Prado Museum, and then spend the night partying in the city's many clubs and bars. This package includes round trip airfare to Madrid as well as opportunities to visit the Prado Museum, the Royal Palace, and the Santiago Bernabeu Stadium. We also offer day trips to Toledo and Segovia for an added cost.

Barcelona

Do you like the beach? Do you want to try some of the best food in the world? If so, Barcelona is the place for you! Barcelona is a beautiful city located on the Mediterranean Sea. It is the capital of the Catalonia region of Spain and is known for its beautiful beaches, its architecture, and its vibrant nightlife. This package includes round trip airfare to Barcelona as well as opportunities to visit the Sagrada Familia, the Picasso Museum, and the Camp Nou Stadium. We also offer day trips to Girona and Tarragona for an added cost.

Granada

Do you like history? Do you want to see the Alhambra? If so, Granada is the place for you! Granada is a beautiful city located in the Andalusia region of Spain. It is known for its Moorish architecture and its beautiful Alhambra Palace. This package includes round trip airfare to Granada as well as opportunities to visit the Alhambra Palace, the Generalife Gardens, and the Cathedral of Granada. We also offer day trips to Cordoba and Seville for an added cost.
